How I Ended Up Reading People's Life Blueprints

Back in 2022, a friend asked me about Human Design.
I was an undergraduate student studying Pharmacy, with a passion for esotericism, analyzing and trading crypto charts for fun.

So when she showed me this system that mixed astrology, I Ching, chakras, and quantum physics into one framework... I figured it was either brilliant or completely insane.

Turned out to be both.

See, I've always been the person who can't just use something. I need to reverse-engineer it. Take it apart. Understand the wiring underneath. Whether that's a video game character's skill tree, a market pattern, or apparently, the operating system of a human being.

So I dove in.

Started with my own chart. Then my family's. Friends. Random people who'd let me experiment on them. And something weird kept happening - people would cry. Not sad crying. The "holy shit someone finally sees me" kind.
