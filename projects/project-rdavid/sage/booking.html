Common Questions

What if I don't know my exact birth time?

That's okay. An approximation works, though exact time gives more accurate results. Some chart elements remain the same regardless of birth time.

Can I book for someone else?

Yes! Just provide their birth details. They should ideally be present for the reading, but it's not required.

Do I need to prepare anything?

Just your birth details and an open mind. Come with questions if you have them, but not required.

Can I reschedule?

Yes. Life happens. Just give at least 24 hours notice and we'll find another time.
